About Us

This is an internet hobby store based in Sri Lanka. We have a myriad of different itens that they sell including ATV's, electronics and collectible items.

MISSION

arngren is guided by four principles: customer obsession rather than competitor focus, passion for invention, commitment to operational excellence, and long-term thinking.

VISION

We strive to have a positive impact on customers, employees, small businesses, the economy, and communities. arngrenies are smart, passionate builders with different backgrounds and goals, who share a common desire to always be learning and inventing on behalf of our customers.

PASSION

The arngren Goes Gold campaign is all about raising awareness of childhood cancer. We asked an Amazonian and his daughter to share what they learned about facing a grim diagnosis and how people who want to help can make a difference.
